Discover New World

History

New World was created in 2021 and is a merging of two old servers : New York Roleplay and Plugin France. After the merge, it was led by Lofuzi, Mvgos and Mylann. After leaving the project to focus in their personal life, Mvgos is now the only founder of New World.

Server Presentation

New World is a roleplay server on FiveM, based on the map of GTA 4.

Staff Team

New World staff team consists of GameMasters, whom controls the game from inside, a staff team to resolve any problem you might ecounter in game, and a support team to resolve problems on the discord.

GTA 4 est un jeu vidéo representé dans la ville de New York. On peut ainsi admirer dans le jeu les monuments célèbres de New York, comme ici Times Square.
